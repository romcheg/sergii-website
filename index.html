About

Dedicated to Precision.
Committed to Recovery.

Dr. Sergii Chagovets is a board-certified orthopaedic surgeon with fellowship training in upper extremity surgery. He completed his medical degree at the National Medical University in Kyiv, followed by residency in Orthopaedic Surgery and a subspecialty fellowship in Hand and Upper Extremity Surgery at a leading European academic centre.

Over more than 15 years of clinical practice, Dr. Chagovets has built a reputation for meticulous surgical technique, clear patient communication, and a compassionate approach to care. His subspecialty focus on the shoulder, elbow, wrist, and hand allows him to offer highly specialised expertise for both common and complex upper-limb conditions.

Dr. Chagovets is an active member of the American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons (AAOS) and the American Society for Surgery of the Hand (ASSH). He regularly contributes to peer-reviewed literature and presents at international conferences, ensuring his practice reflects the latest advances in the field.

Areas of Expertise

Upper Extremity Specializations

Dr. Chagovets's subspecialty focus spans the entire upper limb — from the shoulder to the fingertips — allowing comprehensive, coordinated care.

Shoulder

The shoulder is the most mobile joint in the body — and one of the most injury-prone. Dr. Chagovets treats the full spectrum of shoulder pathology, from instability to advanced arthritis.

  • — Rotator cuff tears & tendinopathy
  • — Shoulder instability & dislocation
  • — Glenohumeral & AC joint arthritis
  • — SLAP lesions & labral tears
  • — Frozen shoulder (adhesive capsulitis)

Elbow

Elbow conditions can significantly impair work and daily activities. Whether from overuse, injury, or degeneration, Dr. Chagovets offers targeted treatment to restore pain-free movement.

  • — Tennis & golfer's elbow (epicondylitis)
  • — Cubital tunnel syndrome
  • — Distal biceps & triceps tears
  • — Elbow arthritis & stiffness
  • — Elbow fractures & instability

Wrist

The wrist is a complex joint essential for nearly every hand function. Dr. Chagovets specialises in both acute wrist injuries and chronic degenerative conditions.

  • — Carpal tunnel syndrome
  • — Scaphoid fractures & non-union
  • — TFCC tears & wrist ligament injuries
  • — Distal radius fractures
  • — Wrist arthritis & ganglion cysts

Hand

Hand conditions affect quality of life profoundly. Dr. Chagovets's microsurgical training enables him to address intricate hand pathology with precision and care.

  • — Dupuytren's contracture
  • — Trigger finger (stenosing tenosynovitis)
  • — Flexor & extensor tendon repair
  • — Thumb base arthritis (CMC joint)
  • — Finger fractures & joint injuries

Patient Information

For Our Patients

We are committed to a transparent and supportive patient experience — from your first call to the final stage of rehabilitation.

01

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment typically lasts 45–60 minutes. Dr. Chagovets will review your history, examine you thoroughly, and discuss any imaging or test results. You will leave with a clear understanding of your diagnosis and a recommended treatment plan.

02

Treatment & Surgery

If surgery is recommended, every aspect of the procedure will be explained clearly, including risks, benefits, and alternatives. We coordinate with anaesthesia, hospital, and allied health teams to ensure a seamless, safe surgical experience.

03

Rehabilitation

Recovery is as important as the procedure itself. Dr. Chagovets works closely with our network of specialist physiotherapists and hand therapists to design a personalised rehabilitation programme that maximises your functional recovery.

Preparing for Your Consultation

  • Bring a photo ID and your insurance card or payment details.
  • Bring any X-ray, MRI, or CT images and reports (digital or physical).
  • Write down a list of all current medications, including supplements.
  • Note the onset, character, and progression of your symptoms.
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing for easy examination.
  • Consider bringing a family member or friend for support and note-taking.

Insurance & Access

We accept most major private health insurance plans. Please contact our office to verify your specific coverage before your appointment. Our administrative team will assist with authorisation and claims.

Self-pay patients are warmly welcomed. Transparent pricing is provided upfront, and payment plans may be arranged. Please call or email us to discuss options.

Our clinic is wheelchair accessible. If you require interpreter services, please let us know when booking — we accommodate multiple languages including Ukrainian and Russian.
